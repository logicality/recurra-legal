Privacy Policy

Recurra is designed with privacy in mind. This page summarizes how the app handles information.

Accounts and data

Recurra does not require an account. Your task data is stored locally on your device.

How we use your information

We do not sell your personal data. The app does not show ads. Recurra does not track you across other apps or websites for advertising or analytics purposes.

Purchases

Payment or unlock status for optional features may be processed through Apple’s In-App Purchase system and, where applicable, services such as RevenueCat for purchase validation and management. Apple’s privacy practices apply to that flow.

Deletion

You can remove app data from your device by deleting the app, subject to your iPhone settings. See Support for a short guide.
