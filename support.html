Support

Recurra helps you keep recurring life-maintenance tasks visible and easy to act on, with a simple, local-first design—no sign-up, no cloud required for your task list.

FAQ

How do I recharge a task?

When a task is ready, use the in-app control for that task (for example, press and hold the ring or follow the on-screen action) to mark it as done and recharge it for the next cycle.

Where is my data stored?

Your tasks and app data are stored on your iPhone. Recurra does not require an account for this. Optional purchase status may be verified through Apple and related services, but your task list stays on the device as described in our Privacy Policy.

How do I restore purchases?

On your iPhone, use the “Restore purchases” or equivalent option in the app if you’ve upgraded before, or use Apple’s standard restore flow for the same Apple ID. If something doesn’t restore as expected, contact us below with your purchase context (we can’t see your Apple receipts).

How do I delete my data?

Delete Recurra from your device. That removes the app and its local data, subject to how iOS manages app storage. We do not hold a copy of your tasks on our servers in the way this app is described on this site.
